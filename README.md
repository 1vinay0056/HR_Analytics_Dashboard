## 1. Project Title / Headline

### 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard: Employee Attrition & Workforce Insights

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and job-role trends. The dashboard helps HR professionals identify key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven workforce management decisions.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The HR Analytics Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, salary trends, and job roles. It enables HR teams and business leaders to monitor employee retention, identify risk factors, and improve organizational performance through data-driven decision-making.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Primary platform for creating interactive reports and dashboards.
• 📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
• 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic analytics.
• 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships among HR datasets for efficient reporting and filtering.
• 📈 **Data Visualization** – Interactive charts, KPI cards, slicers, and tables for workforce analysis.
• 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.pdf/.png` for dashboard sharing and previews.

---

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** HR Employee Attrition Dataset

The dataset contains employee-related information such as:

* Employee demographics (Age, Gender, Education)
* Salary details
* Years at company
* Job roles and departments
* Attrition status
* Workforce distribution metrics

The data is structured to support attrition analysis, employee segmentation, and workforce performance evaluation.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### • Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational productivity, recruitment costs, and workforce stability. HR teams often struggle to identify the key factors contributing to employee turnover.

Important questions include:

* Which age groups experience the highest attrition?
* What salary ranges show the highest employee turnover?
* Which job roles are most affected by attrition?
* Does education level influence attrition rates?
* How does tenure impact employee retention?

---

### • Goal of the Dashboard

To provide an interactive analytical solution that:

* Monitors employee attrition trends.
* Identifies high-risk employee segments.
* Supports retention strategy planning.
* Enables HR departments to make informed workforce decisions.
* Improves employee satisfaction and organizational performance.

### • Business Impact & Insights

✅ **Employee Retention Improvement:** HR teams can identify high-risk employee groups and develop targeted retention strategies.

✅ **Compensation Optimization:** Salary-based attrition analysis helps organizations review compensation structures.

✅ **Workforce Planning:** Understanding attrition by role and tenure supports better recruitment and succession planning.

✅ **Data-Driven HR Decisions:** Leadership can leverage workforce insights to improve employee engagement and reduce turnover costs.

✅ **Performance Monitoring:** Continuous tracking of HR KPIs helps measure the effectiveness of retention initiatives.
